Privacy Policy

TL;DR: Sendvia doesn't collect, store, or sell your data. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Your files transfer directly between devices.

At Sendvia, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your data when you use our file sharing service.

What We Don't Collect

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Device information or browser fingerprints
  • User accounts or profiles

No File Storage

Your files are never uploaded to our servers. All transfers happen directly between sender and receiver using peer-to-peer technology (WebRTC). We cannot access, view, or store your files.

No Cookies

Sendvia does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any persistent storage mechanisms to identify or track users.

No Analytics or Tracking

We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking services. We have no idea who uses our service or how often.

What We Temporarily Store

To enable peer-to-peer connections, we temporarily store minimal signaling data:

  • Random 6-character transfer codes (generated locally, not linked to any user)
  • WebRTC signaling information to establish connections

This data is automatically deleted within minutes after the transfer completes or fails. It contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify users.

Third-Party Services

Sendvia does not integrate with any third-party services that could collect your data. The only external resources loaded are:

  • Google Fonts (for typography) - loaded without user tracking
  • QR Code generation library (runs entirely in your browser)

Data Security

All file transfers are encrypted end-to-end using WebRTC's built-in encryption (DTLS-SRTP). Only the sender and receiver can access the transferred files.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there's nothing for you to access, correct, or delete. You maintain complete control over your data at all times.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle data (which we don't plan to), we will update this policy and notify users through the website.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can review our open-source code on GitHub or reach out through our repository.
